About us

Astraco Freight Forwarders is one of the pioneering freight forwarding company in Malaysia having been established in 1979 and based in Ipoh, Perak. From then, we have grown to be a comprehensive logistics service provider; operating our own bonded warehouse, fleet of transport, marine insurance agency and our own packing team.

Thereafter, acknowledging the need for expansion, Astraco Logistics was established in 1999 to cater for the overseas market; offering highly exclusive freight rates to Inter Asia sector and having a network of international forwarders for our client’s door to door arrangements. Located in Klang which is the No.1 seaport in Malaysia, we are even better equipped to cater to clients in the international spectrum. 

At Astraco, we pride ourselves with our extensive knowledge in freight forwarding, handling all sorts of cargoes from oversized cargo to dangerous goods. With clients in the E&E sector, F&B sector, consumer goods, engineering and commodity-based products such as Steel, Palm Oil, Wood-based industries, we are able to advise our clients on their respective industry’s best practices in terms of logistics and handling.

Having said that, our company remains lean in our manpower but still committed to providing our clients with a ‘personal touch’ as we believe no two companies are the same. As the saying “Seeing is Believing”, we have adopted the motto of “SEA what we can do!” so our clients can judge for themselves our CAPABILITIES, COMMITMENT AND CONSISTENCY!

Fumigation Services

Container fumigation is the treatment of a commodity or the container itself to eliminate the risk of pests or disease from entering or leaving the country. Fumigation provides an effective results and to protect your business from preventing or curing pest infestations.
Shipping container fumigation is cital to rid cargo of pests before the container reaches its final destination. Ridding cargoes of insects and their eggs prevents infestation when the cargo reaches its doorstep such as factories, warehouse, shop or end users. Another reason why shipping container fumigation is necessary, it is to stop cross borders contamintation of pests and to prevent the spreads of disease and other contamination.

Dangerous / Hazardous Cargo

Dangerous / Hazardous Cargo are often known as hazardous materials which may be pure chemicals. mixtures of substances, manufactured product or items wich can pose a risk to people, animals, infrastructures, environment if not well handle in use or in trasnportation.
Due to it hazardous nature, dangerous shipments are regulated under stringent rules and regulation throughout the entire process of transportation.
Therefore, our team of dedicated professionals are providing strategic solutions for safety handling and shipping of the dangerous cargo.
